# Psytrance Internal EQ Cleanup

Internal Mixer-EQ is **not a true steep highpass**.
Band 0 should be considered a low-shelf cleanup, not a true lowcut.
These values are template starting points, not final mix rules.

## Template Suggestions
- Leads & Arps: -12 dB @ approx. 180–250 Hz
- Pads & Atmos: -12 dB @ approx. 200–300 Hz
- Vocals: -12 dB @ approx. 120–180 Hz
- Hats: -18 dB @ approx. 300–500 Hz
- Percussion: -12 dB @ approx. 150–300 Hz
- Snare/Clap: -12 dB @ approx. 100–150 Hz
- Bass: -6 dB @ approx. 20–30 Hz
- Kick: -3 to -6 dB @ approx. 20–30 Hz

## Notes
- For true lowcuts, it is better to use Fruity Parametric EQ 2, MAutoDynamicEQ, TDR Nova, or ZL Equalizer 2.
- In F# minor, F#1 is roughly at 46.25 Hz and F#2 is roughly at 92.50 Hz; do not thin out Bass/Kick in the musical fundamental frequency range.
